Batta

Batta is a Village in Kharar Tehsil in S.a.s Nagar District of Punjab State, India. It is located 20 KM towards west from District head quarters Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar. 12 KM from Kharar. 25 KM from State capital Chandigarh
Batta Pin code is 140413 and postal head office is Gharuan .
Sil Kapra ( 1 KM ) , Rora ( 2 KM ) , Garangan ( 2 KM ) , Ghoga Kheri ( 2 KM ) , Shakrullapur ( 2 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Batta.
Batta is surrounded by Bassi Pathana Tehsil towards west , Kurali Tehsil towards North , Kharar Tehsil towards East , Khera Tehsil towards South .
Morinda , Kharar , Kurali , Mohali are the near by Cities to Batta.
This Place is in the border of the S.a.s Nagar District and Rupnagar District.
Rupnagar District Kurali is North towards this place . Also it is in the Border of other district Chandigarh . It is near to the Chandigarh State Border.
Batta 2011 Census Details Batta Local Language is Punjabi.
Batta Village Total population is 1152 and number of houses are 217. Female Population is 45.3%.
Village literacy rate is 75.2% and the Female Literacy rate is 31.3%.
