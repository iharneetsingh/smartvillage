Rurkee

Rurkee Kham is a medium size village located in Kharar Tehsil of Mohali district, Punjab with total 216 families residing. The Rurkee Kham village has population of 1164 of which 613 are males while 551 are females as per Population Census 2011.
In Rurkee Kham village population of children with age 0-6 is 105 which makes up 9.02 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Rurkee Kham village is 899 which is higher than Punjab state average of 895. Child Sex Ratio for the Rurkee Kham as per census is 615, lower than Punjab average of 846.
Rurkee Kham village has higher literacy rate compared to Punjab. In 2011, literacy rate of Rurkee Kham village was 80.55 % compared to 75.84 % of Punjab. In Rurkee Kham Male literacy stands at 84.85 % while female literacy rate was 75.93 %.
